Welcome to our Diversity Dialogues 2024: Remaking Theater of the Oppressed for our times.
In February 2023 about 20 theater of the oppressed practitioners, or jokers as they are called in TO language, gathered in Bangalore to ask themselves:
How is TO responding to the challanges of our times? A methodology created in Brazil 50 years ago, and in response to a specific political situation, is TO relevant in the Indian context today?
The answer is YES! More than ever before, in fact. We are surrounded by divisive and dehumanizing discourses. We are being pressured in an inentional, calculated manner, to stay in silos, compartmentalize our lives and thoughts, and our work, so that our voices stay weak. More than ever.
